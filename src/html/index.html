Widening income gap

Of the 53 Florida counties with reliable income data, 44 saw an increase in the gap between the wealthiest residents and the poorest — those in the 95th and 20th percentiles — from 2007 to 2014.

Nationwide this income gap grew by 7.7 percent during the same period and Florida's grew by 11.6 percent.

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
