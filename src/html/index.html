Wages struggle to rebound

In 42 of 67 Florida counties, average annual wages — adjusted for inflation — per employee declined from 2007 to 2015. Although these wages grew nationally by 4.2 percent during that period (from $50,821 to $ 52,937 ), the increase in Florida has only been 1.8 percent (from $45,435 to $46,245).

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
