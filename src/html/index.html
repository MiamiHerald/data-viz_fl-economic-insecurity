Increased poverty rates

The poverty rate increased in 50 of Florida's 53 counties with reliable data from 2007 to 2014, meaning only three counties have less poverty now than before the recession. Statewide the poverty rate rose 32.7 percent (from 12.6 to 16.7 percent), and nationally it rose 17.6 percent (from 13.3 to 15.6 percent).

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
