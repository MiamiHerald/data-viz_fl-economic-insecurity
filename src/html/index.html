Employment levels lag behind

While the national employment growth rate was 3 percent, Florida's was only 1.2 percent from 2007 to 2015. In 40 of Florida's 67 counties, total employment levels decreased from 2007 to 2015, meaning jobs lost during the recession have not been replaced.

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
