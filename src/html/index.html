Increased poverty rates

The poverty rate increased in 36 of Florida's 42 counties with reliable data from 2007 to 2015. Statewide the poverty rate rose 25 percent (from 12.6 to 15.7 percent), and nationally it rose 11 percent (from 13.3 to 14.7 percent).

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
