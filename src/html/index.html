Wages struggle to rebound after recession

In 40 of 67 Florida counties, average annual wages — adjusted for inflation — per employee declined from 2007 to 2015. Although these wages grew nationally by 4.2 percent during that period, the increase in Florida has only been 1.8 percent.

Sources: Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages, Bureau of Labor Statistics; Florida International University Metropolitan Center
